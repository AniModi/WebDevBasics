Mahatma Gandhi

The man who fought without violence

Here is timeline of Mahatma Gandhi's Life:

  • 1869 - Born in Porbandar in Gujarat
  • 1888 - Leaves for England to study law.
  • 1920 - Calls for non corporation movement
  • 1922 - Arrested for sedition
  • 1925 - Becomes president of INC
  • 1931 - Begins the famous Salt March
  • 1931 - Arrested for violation salt law, non corporation movement breaks out nationwide
  • 1931 - Released, participates in round table conference
  • 1932 - Again arrested for sedition, without trial
  • 1932 - Protests in prison for rights of untouchables
  • 1942 - British offers Dominion Status, rejects, arrested
  • 1944 - Visits Jinnah, unable to work out an agreement to keep nation whole
  • 1947 - India gets independence, partitioned
  • 1948 - Assassinated by Nathuram Vinayuk Godse
“Be the change that you wish to see in the world.”
--Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi
